Get to know me.

Hi, I'm Julian. I am a front end developer with a passion in UI/UX Design. I was born and raised in Fort Worth, TX. I have spent the majority of my adult life in Austin, TX after graduating from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in Mexican American Studies, B.A.

Philosophy on Design: I follow the ideation that "less is more." Web design should be clean, yet highly accessible to all types of clients. I don't want my viewers to be discouraged with my work because they can't maintain focus on the content.
