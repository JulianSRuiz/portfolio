Alien Hunt: Group Project

I presented the idea of recreating a timeless classic Nintendo game for a group project at DevMountain. This was a fantastic experience working with others and seeing how different minds come together. I focused on project management ensuring that everyone maintained their respective responsibilities as well as the positioning and scaling of nearly everything you see in this game. I presented the idea of working with PixiJS and after only three days of hands on experience with the newly introduced rendering library, we were up and running with the beginning bits of a functioning game.
